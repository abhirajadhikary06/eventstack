About EventStack

Welcome to EventStack — your ultimate tool for streamlined event planning. Whether you're hosting a tech meetup, study group, or casual get-together, EventStack lets you organize everything collaboratively and in real-time.

With real-time voting, intuitive scheduling, and dynamic participant management, our platform keeps everyone in sync. No more endless chats to decide a date—EventStack brings clarity to chaos.

We’ve built this using Tornado, making the platform incredibly responsive, scalable, and fast. You get live updates as votes roll in—no page reloads necessary.

Our design philosophy revolves around transparency, collaboration, and simplicity. We believe that planning events should feel fun and easy—not frustrating.

EventStack is proudly open-source. Got ideas or want to contribute? Jump right in. We love building with the community.

Explore the code or star us on GitHub. Let’s create better events together.
