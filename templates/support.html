Support

Have questions, feedback, or facing issues? The EventStack team is here to help! We’re an open-source project built by a passionate community, and we appreciate your interest in reaching out.

1. Contact Us

For general support, bug reports, or feature suggestions, drop us an email at loominx@outlook.com

2. GitHub Issues

Found a bug or want to suggest a feature? Please create an issue on our GitHub repository. Provide as much detail as possible, and we’ll do our best to address it promptly.

3. Contributing

Interested in contributing to EventStack? Check out our repository on GitHub. Whether you're fixing bugs, designing interfaces, or writing docs—every bit counts!

4. FAQs

  • Is EventStack free to use? — Yes! It’s completely free and open-source.
  • Can I deploy my own version? — Absolutely. Fork the repo and go ahead!
  • How do I report a bug? — Open an issue on GitHub with details and steps to reproduce.

We appreciate your support and interest in EventStack. Together, we’re building a platform that makes coordination fun, transparent, and effortless.
